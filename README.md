# masjid-accounting-software

Problem Statement:
--
Financial systems were not built with everyone in mind. For Muslim communities, this is worse. Donations disappear into opaque institutions. Credit systems penalise businesses through interest-based structures. Capital is poured out of Muslim communities into unethical systems, while many products designed to help violate the very principles of the people they claim to serve. 
Muslim communities generate significant wealth through work, trade, and giving. But without infrastructure to circulate that wealth ethically, transparently, and directly towards community benefit, it leaks into systems that were never built for us.

**How might we build the financial infrastructure our communities actually need: systems that invest in sustainably building Muslim communities, are transparent by design, and treat Islamic principles as a foundation?**
--



Our solution:
We are building an all-in-one financial management platform for masjids and Muslim charities.

Today, donations often come in through different channels, for different causes, and with different religious or donor restrictions. A donor might give Zakat, Sadaqah, or money for a specific campaign like a wudu-area renovation or youth event. But once the payment is collected, organisations can struggle to clearly track what each pound was intended for, how much sits in each restricted pot, and whether spending matches the donor’s intention.

Our solution captures the purpose of a donation at the point of payment. When someone donates, they select the charity type, such as Zakat or Sadaqah, and the specific cause they want to support. On the admin side, the organisation instantly sees donations organised into clear financial pots: Zakat, Sadaqah, campaign funds, project funds, and general donations.

This gives masjids and Muslim charities a simple treasury dashboard where they can track income, understand available funds, respect donor restrictions, and make better financial decisions.

Our prototype focuses on two core workflows: a donation page that captures the type and purpose of every donation, and an admin dashboard that automatically separates those donations into the right pots. In the future, this can expand into expense approvals, restricted-fund spending checks, forecasting, reporting, Gift Aid, accounting integrations, and full treasury management.

In short: we help Muslim organisations collect, separate, track, and manage donations with clarity and trust.
